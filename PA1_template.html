Reproducible Research: Peer Assessment 1

Data collected from a personal device that keeps track of how many steps an individual takes can offer interesting insight into their patterns of activity over time. In this case, the data was collected over the period from 2012-10-01 to 2012-11-30. Some of the data was missing, and was imputed in order to create a complete set.
